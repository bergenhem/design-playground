About

Hello there traveler, why don't you sit a while a listen?

As you might've guessed my name is Carl Bergenhem. I am a Boston-based web developer, technical speaker, and gaming aficionado.

So, what do I do? Well, I currently work as the Manager of the Solutions Consultant Team at Telerik, an awesome software company that specializes in providing developer tools for the entire end-to-end development application life cycle. I'm lucky enough to be able to focus on web-related technologies, specifically with our ASP.NET Ajax, ASP.NET MVC, and pure client-side products.

This might be obvious, but I love the web. You can often find me going on-and-on about how the web is the ideal platform to use, and I try to share my affection to this lovely collection of technologies by speaking at various conferences.
